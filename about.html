About BigPlanetarium

A Bristol-based educational planetarium making space science accessible and exciting for absolutely everyone.

Our Mission

Science is for everyone

Education for All

We work with schools across Bristol and the South West to deliver curriculum-linked planetarium sessions. Our dome shows bring the Solar System to life in ways no textbook can.

Public Shows

Evening and weekend planetarium shows for curious adults and families. From introductory tours of the night sky to specialist talks on black holes and deep space exploration.

Accessible Online

This website follows WCAG 2.2 accessibility guidelines. We prioritise clear language, strong contrast, and compatibility with assistive technologies so anyone can explore space from home.

Who We Are

A small team with a big passion for space

Our Venue in Bristol

Our physical venue is fully accessible with step-free access throughout, accessible toilets, designated seating areas, and hearing loops. We welcome visitors of all abilities.

Accessibility First

Accessibility is at the heart of everything we do. Our digital content follows WCAG 2.2 guidelines, and our team is trained to support visitors with a wide range of needs.

If you have any specific requirements, please contact us in advance so we can ensure your visit is as comfortable as possible.
