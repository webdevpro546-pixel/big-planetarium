Accessibility Information

We welcome all visitors

Wheelchair Access

Step-free entrance throughout the venue. Accessible toilets on every floor. Designated wheelchair spaces in the planetarium dome.

Hearing Support

Hearing loops available in all presentation spaces. Please ask at reception upon arrival.

Visual Impairment

Large print guides available. Guide dogs welcome. Audio-described shows available on request.
